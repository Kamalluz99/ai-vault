## daily news — 06-04-26

> [!note]- AI — Trump signs executive order for pre-release government access to AI models
> The Trump administration reversed its anti-regulation stance and signed an EO requiring AI companies to **voluntarily submit their most powerful models for government testing up to 30 days before public release**. The shift was triggered by Anthropic's April decision to limit its *Mythos Preview* model after it demonstrated the ability to identify and exploit software vulnerabilities. Companies are not legally compelled — the order relies on voluntary cooperation from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google — but the signal is clear: frontier models are now a national security matter.
> Source: [NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/02/nx-s1-5844347/ai-safety-trump-executive-order) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/02/trump-executive-order-ai.html)
