**Fajr** (الفجر) is the first of the five daily obligatory prayers in Islam, performed before sunrise. [[Dr. Omar Suleiman]] calls establishing Fajr in congregation (jama'ah) "breaking the spell" — it is the single most transformative anchor of the Prophet's ﷺ daily routine. See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
