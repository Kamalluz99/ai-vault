Anki is a free, open-source flashcard application built around [[spaced repetition]] — an evidence-based learning technique that schedules card reviews at increasing intervals as you demonstrate recall. It is widely used by medical students, language learners, and anyone needing long-term retention of large fact sets. Cards can contain text, images, audio, and LaTeX. AnkiWeb syncs decks across devices. In the [[Reysu]] ai-life-skills vault setup, an [[Obsidian]] plugin sends highlights from summary notes directly to Anki as flashcards, bridging passive reading with active recall.
