**Dhul Hijjah** (ذو الحجة) is the twelfth and final month of the [[Hijri calendar]]. It contains the first ten days — among the most virtuous days of the year — the Day of Arafah (9th), Eid al-Adha (10th), and the days of Tashreeq. The Hajj pilgrimage takes place in this month. See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
