**Bir Rumah** (بئر رومة, Well of Rumah) was a water well in [[Medina]] that belonged to a man named Rumah. The Prophet ﷺ announced: *"Whoever buys the well of Rumah and makes it available to Muslims, I guarantee him Jannah."* [[Uthman ibn Affan]] purchased it and made it freely available to the Muslim community — one of his most celebrated acts of sadaqah jariyah. See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
