**Ramadan** (رمضان) is the ninth month of the Islamic [[Hijri calendar]] and the month of obligatory fasting. It is the most widely recognised Islamic month — [[Dr. Omar Suleiman]] notes that most Muslims only know the Hijri date when it is Ramadan, Sha'ban, or Dhul Hijjah. See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
