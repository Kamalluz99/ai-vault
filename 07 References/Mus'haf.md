**Mus'haf** (مصحف) refers to a physical written copy of the Quran. During the caliphate of [[Uthman ibn Affan]], he oversaw the standardisation and mass distribution of a single authoritative recitation of the Quran — burning variant copies to prevent confusion. This "Uthmani Mus'haf" is the standard Quranic text read worldwide today. See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
