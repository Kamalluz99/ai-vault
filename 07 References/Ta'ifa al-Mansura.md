Al-Ta'ifa al-Mansura (الطائفة المنصورة — the Victorious/Triumphant Group) is a concept from hadith in which the Prophet ﷺ describes a group from his Ummah that will always remain steadfast on truth, not harmed by those who oppose or betray them, until the Final Hour. Multiple hadith place this group in [[Al-Sham]], particularly around [[Jerusalem]]. They are described as holding down the frontier of Islam, not deterred by betrayal or abandonment by others. Some scholars identify them with the scholars of aqeedah who guard Islamic belief; others with the warriors defending the borders; most commonly both. Their resilience — especially amid betrayal — is a defining characteristic.
