Yaqeen Institute for Islamic Research is a US-based nonprofit Islamic research organisation founded and led by [[Dr. Omar Suleiman]], headquartered in [[Dallas, TX]]. Its mission is to address doubts about Islam and build conviction (*yaqeen* = certainty) through rigorous, accessible research.

**Key outputs:**
- Long-form academic papers on Islamic theology, ethics, and contemporary issues
- The [[Al-Aqsa Series]] — lecture series on the virtues and history of [[Masjid al-Aqsa]]
- [[The Firsts]] — companion biography series breaking down the lives of the Sahaba
- Free downloadable books on Islamic practice and spirituality
- MOSIN — a Yaqeen-affiliated nonprofit supporting families with special needs children

**Notable facts:**
- All content is free in some form (Dr. Suleiman's policy)
- Dr. Suleiman is listed in the [[Muslim 500]] as one of the world's most influential Muslims
