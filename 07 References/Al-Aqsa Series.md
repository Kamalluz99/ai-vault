The Al-Aqsa Series is a 10-lecture course by [[Dr. Omar Suleiman]], produced by [[Yaqeen Institute]] and delivered at the Minan Center. It provides a comprehensive Islamic treatment of [[Masjid Al-Aqsa]] and [[Al-Quds]] (Jerusalem) — covering their virtues (fadha'il), history, prophetic significance, connection to Islamic eschatology, and the theological obligation of Muslims toward [[Palestine]]. Lecture 1 covers the virtues of Al-Quds through Quranic and hadith evidence. The series runs across 10 weekly sessions, building from foundational concepts toward a complete picture by the final lecture.
