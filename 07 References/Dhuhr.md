**Dhuhr** (الظهر) is the second obligatory daily prayer, performed at midday. A sunnah dua exists specifically for Wednesdays, to be made between Dhuhr and Asr — one of the day-specific prophetic practices highlighted in [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
