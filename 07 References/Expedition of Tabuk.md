**The Expedition of Tabuk** (غزوة تبوك, also called Jaish al-Usra — the Army of Hardship) was the last major military campaign led by the Prophet ﷺ, in 9 AH (630 CE), against the Byzantine Empire. It was particularly difficult due to extreme heat and the reluctance of some Muslims to participate. [[Uthman ibn Affan]] financed the entire expedition single-handedly, prompting the Prophet ﷺ to say: *"Nothing will harm Uthman after what he did today."* See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
