Palestine (فلسطين, Filastin) is a historical and geographic region at the eastern Mediterranean, encompassing what is today Israel, the West Bank, and Gaza. In Islam it is referred to as the blessed land (al-ard al-mubarakah), the land of the prophets, the land of the olives ([[Surah at-Teen]]), and the Sham region — the original land of [[Hijra]] to which prophets fled. Since 1948 (Nakba) and especially since 1967, the majority of Palestinians have been dispossessed, living either under military occupation, blockade ([[Gaza]]), or as refugees. The Palestinian cause holds unique theological weight in Islam: [[Al-Quds]] and [[Masjid Al-Aqsa]] are among its most sacred sites, and their liberation is considered an obligation of the Muslim Ummah.
