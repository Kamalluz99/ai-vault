Obsidian is a local-first markdown note-taking app built around the concept of a personal knowledge base. Notes are stored as plain `.md` files on your device — you own the data. Its core feature is bidirectional linking via `[[wikilinks]]`, which creates a graph of connected ideas visible in the built-in graph view. Obsidian has a rich plugin ecosystem (community and core) including [[Dataview]] for database-style queries, [[Daily Notes]], [[Canvas]], and [[Bases]]. It is the primary vault format used by [[Claude Code]] AI-managed second brain setups like the [[ai-life-skills]] template.
