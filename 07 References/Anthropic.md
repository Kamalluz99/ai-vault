Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021 by [[Dario Amodei]], [[Daniela Amodei]], and other former [[OpenAI]] researchers. It develops the [[Claude]] family of [[LLM]]s, built around a safety methodology called Constitutional AI (CAI). Anthropic's products include the Claude consumer app, the Anthropic API (used by [[Claude Code]] and developers), and research into AI interpretability and alignment. The API applies stricter data-handling guarantees than the consumer subscription: data is retained for one week and not used for model training — a meaningful privacy distinction for sensitive workloads.
