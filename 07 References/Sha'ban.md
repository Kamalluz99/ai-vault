**Sha'ban** (شعبان) is the eighth month of the [[Hijri calendar]], immediately preceding [[Ramadan]]. The Prophet ﷺ fasted more in Sha'ban than any other month besides Ramadan. It is one of the three Hijri months most Muslims can identify by name. See [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
