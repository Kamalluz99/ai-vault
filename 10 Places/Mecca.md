**Mecca** (مكة المكرمة, Makkah al-Mukarramah) is the holiest city in Islam, located in present-day Saudi Arabia. It is the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and the site of [[Masjid Al-Haram]] and the Kaaba. Muslims face Mecca in prayer and are required to perform Hajj there once in a lifetime if able.
