**Medina** (المدينة المنورة, al-Madinah al-Munawwarah, "the Radiant City") is the second holiest city in Islam. The Prophet ﷺ migrated there during the [[Hijrah]] (622 CE) and built the first mosque ([[Masjid al-Nabawi]]). He is buried there. The Prophet's ﷺ body is preserved intact — the earth does not consume the bodies of prophets — and his soul responds to the salaam of every visitor.
