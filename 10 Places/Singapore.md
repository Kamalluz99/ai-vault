**Singapore** is a city-state in Southeast Asia. The lecture [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]] — *A Day in the Life of Our Beloved, Muhammad ﷺ* — was delivered here on 15 September 2025, organised on less than one week's notice. Over 3,000 attendees came, described by Dr. Suleiman as remarkable for the short notice.
