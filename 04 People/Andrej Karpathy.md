> [!info] Andrej Karpathy is a Slovak-Canadian AI researcher, educator, and one of the most influential figures in making deep learning accessible. He was a co-founder of [[OpenAI]], later served as Director of AI at [[Tesla]] leading the Autopilot team, and returned to [[OpenAI]] before going independent in 2023. A PhD graduate from [[Stanford University]] under [[Fei-Fei Li]], Karpathy is best known outside academia for his lecture series (CS231n at Stanford, Neural Networks: Zero to Hero on YouTube), his hands-on educational repos (micrograd, nanoGPT, llm.c, llm.c), and his plain-English explanations of complex AI systems. He is a proponent of building understanding from first principles and regularly publishes practical deep-dives on X and GitHub. His LLM wiki project provides a collection of Obsidian-compatible skills and references for working with large language models.
