> [!info] Musa (as) (Moses) is one of the five greatest Ulul Azm (Prophets of Firm Resolve) in Islam, sent to Banu Isra'il (the Children of Israel). He received the [[Tawrah]] (Torah) on Mount Sinai and led his people out of Egypt. His connection to [[Al-Quds]] is profound: after 40 years leading Banu Isra'il in the desert, he was forbidden by Allah from entering [[Jerusalem]] himself — because the generation of his people had refused to enter and fight. He made du'a to die as close to the holy land as possible, and died at what is identified as Mount Nebo — a stone's throw from the border of [[Jerusalem]], with the soldiers of the enemy at the boundary. He also made [[Hajj]] to Mecca at some point. The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ encountered him during the [[Isra wal-Miraj]].
