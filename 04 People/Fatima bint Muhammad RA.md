> [!info] Fatima bint Muhammad (RA), also known as Fatima al-Zahra (the Radiant), was the youngest and most beloved daughter of the Prophet ﷺ and [[Khadija bint Khuwaylid RA]]. She married [[Ali ibn Abi Talib]] and together they had [[Hasan ibn Ali RA]] and [[Husayn ibn Ali RA]], making her the mother of the Prophet's ﷺ surviving lineage. The Prophet ﷺ said she is the leader of the women of paradise. [[Dr. Omar Suleiman]] notes: *"I would love Fatima without the Prophet telling me to — but I love her because the Prophet ﷺ loved her."* Her qualities of patience, piety, and sacrifice under severe hardship made her a model of faith for all generations.
