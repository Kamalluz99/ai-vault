> [!info]
> Sayyid Ahmad Mashhur al-Haddad was a descendant and spiritual heir of [[Imam Abdallah al-Haddad]], and the primary living authority consulted by translator [[Mostafa al-Badawi]] when difficulties arose in interpreting the text of [[The Book of Assistance]] during its translation. Al-Badawi describes him as "undoubtedly the best-qualified person to comment on his writings." As a carrier of the [[Ba-Alawi]] tradition and direct spiritual heir of al-Haddad, his guidance was considered authoritative in resolving ambiguities between possible interpretations. The translation was brought to completion "entirely through their baraka and assistance."
