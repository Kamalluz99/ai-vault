> [!info]
> Mostafa al-Badawi is a British Muslim scholar and translator of classical Arabic Islamic texts into English. Based in [[Al-Madina al-Munawwara]] (Medina) at the time of the translation, he completed the translation of *[[The Book of Assistance]]* in Ramadan 1408 AH (~1988 CE). He is known for his translations of works from the [[Ba-Alawi]] tradition of [[Hadramaut]], including works by [[Imam Abdallah al-Haddad]]. He worked closely with Sayyid Ahmad Mashhur al-Haddad — described as the spiritual heir of [[Imam Abdallah al-Haddad]] — when difficulties arose in interpreting the text, consulting him as "the best-qualified person to comment on his writings."
