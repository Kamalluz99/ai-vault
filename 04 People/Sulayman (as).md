> [!info] Sulayman (as) (Solomon) was a prophet and king, son of [[Dawud (as)]] (David), given extraordinary wisdom ([[hikma]]), control over wind, jinn, and animals, and a kingdom unlike any before or after. He completed the construction of [[Masjid Al-Aqsa]] in Jerusalem and upon its completion made three famous du'as: (1) wisdom that aligns with Allah's judgment, (2) a kingdom that no one after him would possess (for the sake of da'wah, not vanity), and (3) that whoever comes to [[Masjid Al-Aqsa]] seeking only two rak'ahs for Allah's sake would leave purified of sins like the day they were born. The Quran praises his wisdom extensively. He is also known in Islamic tradition for building over 40 masajid under the general category of [[Masjid Al-Aqsa]].
