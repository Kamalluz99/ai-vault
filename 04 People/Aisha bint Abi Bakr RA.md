> [!info] Aisha bint Abi Bakr (RA) was the daughter of [[Abu Bakr al-Siddiq]] and one of the wives of the Prophet ﷺ, often called "the Mother of the Believers." She is one of the most prolific narrators of hadith — over 2,000 hadiths are attributed to her — and was a respected authority on Islamic jurisprudence, poetry, and medicine in her own right. The Prophet ﷺ described her love for him as a gift from Allah, and said to seek knowledge from her. She lived to approximately 67 years old and was buried in [[Medina]].
