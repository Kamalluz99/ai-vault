> [!info] Maimuna bint al-Harith (ra) was the last wife the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ married, and one of the Mothers of the Believers (Ummahat al-Mu'minin). She was known for her piety and generosity. Referenced in the Al-Aqsa Series lecture: a woman who had recovered from illness and made an oath to pray in [[Masjid Al-Aqsa]] went to consult Maimuna (ra) before setting out. Maimuna conveyed the matter to the Prophet ﷺ, who advised the woman to pray in his masjid in Medina instead — then clarified the relative rewards of each site.
