**Prophet Muhammad ﷺ** (محمد صلى الله عليه وسلم, born ~570 CE in [[Mecca]], died 632 CE in [[Medina]]) is the final prophet and messenger of Allah in Islam. He received the revelation of the Quran over 23 years and established the Muslim community. His biography (seerah), sayings (hadith), and practices (sunnah) form the second primary source of Islamic law and spirituality after the Quran itself. The study of his daily life is the subject of [[09-15-25 Mon Dr Omar Suleiman]].
