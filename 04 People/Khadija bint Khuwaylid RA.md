> [!info] Khadija bint Khuwaylid (RA) was the first wife of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and the first person to accept Islam. A wealthy and respected businesswoman in Mecca, she employed the Prophet ﷺ and later proposed marriage to him. She was his greatest supporter through the early, most difficult years of prophethood — it was in her arms he sought comfort after the first revelation in the Cave of [[Hira]]. The Prophet ﷺ loved her deeply and spoke of her with reverence long after her passing. She is counted among the four greatest women in all of creation alongside [[Fatima bint Muhammad RA]], [[Maryam bint Imran]], and [[Asiya bint Muzahim]].
